# TTB Label Practice Profiles (25)

Twenty-five fictional product profiles (wine, distilled spirits, beer/malt, and
specialty) used as prompts for generating test-label images. Each lists the
structured label data we verify against. Source for the controlled label set and
the eventual `expected-values.csv`.

Field-format notes by type:
- **Wine / mead:** alcohol content as `% VOL.`; net contents in mL.
- **Distilled spirits:** alcohol content as `% Alc./Vol.` **and** `PROOF`.
- **Beer / malt:** alcohol content as `% Alc./Vol.`; net contents usually in US
  fluid measures (with mL equivalent).
- The **Government Health Warning** is mandatory on every profile.
